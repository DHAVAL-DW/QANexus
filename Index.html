Request for Detailed Requirements

Preparing test cases while story description is insufficient

Message Preview
Hi [Assignee/Team], I am currently preparing test cases for this story to ensure comprehensive coverage. However, the description would benefit from additional detail to fully understand the requirements. Could you please update it to include: • Detailed user flows and interactions • Expected behavior and outcomes • Clear acceptance criteria • Any known dependencies or constraints This will help us create accurate and effective test cases. Thank you for your support. Tag: Developer Lead, [Your Lead]

Formal pause for major blockers

Requirement Checklist

  • Clear notes when resuming

Template
Hi [Team], I am placing this ticket on QA Hold temporarily. The validation is dependent on the resolution of the linked bug ([Bug Ticket ID: Title]), and testing prior to its resolution may not yield accurate results. Please update this ticket once the bug is resolved, and we will resume validation promptly. Tag: Developer Lead, [Your Lead]

Moving to Remediation

Template
Hi [Team/Assignee], This story ticket is being moved to Remediation status due to linked bug tickets that require resolution. Please note that validation has been completed from the QA side. Once the linked bugs are resolved, we will be able to proceed with the release of this story. Cc: Developer Lead, [Your Lead])

Stable

Linked bugs exist but do not prevent story completion

Template
Hi [Team], Following our assessment, this story ticket is ready for release. While there are linked bugs pending, they have been evaluated and do not block the completion of this story or prevent it from meeting the defined acceptance criteria. These bugs will be tracked and addressed separately in subsequent iterations. The story functionality has been validated and is ready for production. Tag: Developer Lead, [Your Lead]
