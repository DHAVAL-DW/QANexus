Placing Ticket on QA Hold

Formal pause for major blockers

Requirement Checklist

  • Add explanation to Reviewer Notes
  • Clear notes when resuming
  • Complete scenario validation first
Template
Hello Team, I am placing this ticket on QA Hold temporarily. The validation is dependent on the resolution of the linked bug ([Bug Ticket ID: Title]), and testing prior to its resolution may not yield accurate results. Please update this ticket once the bug is resolved, and we will resume validation promptly. Tag: Developer Lead, [Your Lead]

Template
Hello Team, This story ticket is being moved to Remediation status due to linked bug tickets that require resolution. Please note that validation has been completed from the QA side. Once the linked bugs are resolved, we will be able to proceed with the release of this story. Cc: Developer Lead, [Your Lead])

Linked bugs exist but do not prevent story completion

Template
Hello Team, Following our assessment, this story ticket is ready for release. While there are linked bugs pending, they have been evaluated and do not block the completion of this story or prevent it from meeting the defined acceptance criteria. These bugs will be tracked and addressed separately in subsequent iterations. The story functionality has been validated and is ready for production. Tag: Developer Lead, [Your Lead]
